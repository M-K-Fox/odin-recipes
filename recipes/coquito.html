Coquito

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evapourated milk
  • 2 large egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (14 ounce) can cream of coconut
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • Half cup white rum
  • Half cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Half teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Quater teaspoon ground cloves

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine evapourated milk and egg yolks in the top of a double boiler over simmering water. Cook over the simmering water, stirring constantly, until micture is think enough to coat the back of a spoon, and reaches a temperature of 160 degress F (71 degress C).
  3. Transfer mixture to a blender. Add cream of coconut, sweetened condensed milk, run, water, vanilla, cinnamon, and cloves; blend until combined, about 30 seconds.
  4. Pour into sterilized glass bottles and store in the refrigerator; sever cold or over ice, if desired.
