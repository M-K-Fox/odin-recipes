Coquito

Ingredients

  • Evaporated milk
  • 2 beaten eggs yolks
  • Cream of coconut
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • White rum
  • Water
  • Teaspoon of Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon and Cloves

Directions

  1. Cook the evapourated milk and egg yolks in a double boiler until thick.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Transfer to glass bottles and refrigerator until cold.
